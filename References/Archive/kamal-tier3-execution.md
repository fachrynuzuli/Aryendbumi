### Week 2: Validation & Beta Offer (Days 8-14)

**Goal:** Convert audit respondents to paid beta students, validate Core Offer at Rp 900K

#### Days 8-10: One-on-One Validation Calls

**Action:** Offer free 30-min "Strategy Call" to everyone who said "YES interested in beta"

**Call Framework (30 minutes):**

**Part 1: Emotional Discovery (15 mins)**
- "Tell me about your current career situation"
- "What made you take the audit?" (dig for emotional pain)
- "If nothing changes in next 2 years, what does that look like?" (amplify fear)
- "What have you tried so far?" (show them random tactics don't work)
- "Why is this important to you RIGHT NOW?" (urgency)

**Part 2: Present The Kamal Way Solution (10 mins)**
- "Based on what you shared, here's what I see..." (mirror their pain back)
- "The Kamal Way addresses this specifically through..." (connect to K-A-M-A-L)
- "My 8-week program walks you through..." (explain structure)
- "Other students with similar situations saw..." (social proof)

**Part 3: Beta Offer (5 mins)**
- "I'm opening 10 beta spots for Rp 900K (normally will be Rp 1.5M)"
- "You get: 8 weeks, all modules, community, 4 live calls with me, personal roadmap"
- "Beta students get lifetime access to all future updates"
- "I'm limiting to 10 because I want to personally ensure everyone gets results"
- "Sound like something you want to be part of?"

**If YES:**
- "Awesome! I'll send payment link right after this call. Once paid, I'll add you to our Telegram group and you'll get Week 1 materials immediately."
- Send WhopIO payment link via WhatsApp/Email within 15 minutes
- Follow up within 24h if not paid

**If MAYBE/OBJECTIONS:**
- "What's holding you back?" (listen, don't counter immediately)
- Most common: "I need to think" (=price concern), "Let me talk to spouse" (=not decision maker)
- Use parallel story: "I had another student, [Name], who had same concern. Here's what they realized..."
- "This investment is 3-4 days of your salary. But if it gets you promoted, that's 30-50% increase = pays for itself in 1 month."
- "What would need to be true for this to be a 'hell yes' for you?"

**Target:** 5-10 strategy calls, close 3-5 beta students

**Time Investment:** 2.5-5 hours calls + 2 hours follow-up
