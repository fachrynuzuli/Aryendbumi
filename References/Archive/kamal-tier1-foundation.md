**6. Price:** Rp 1.5M ($100) - 3-4 days of current salary for potential 30-50% lifetime increase

## PART 3: NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

### Rule 1: The Kamal Way Framework is Sacred

**NEVER:**
* ❌ Teach AI without connecting to K-A-M-A-L framework
* ❌ Post content that doesn't tie to framework
* ❌ Let students graduate without understanding all 5 letters
* ❌ Rename or rebrand the framework

**The framework is your moat. Protect it.**

---

### Rule 2: Proof Over Promises

**ALWAYS:**
* ✅ Show real revenue screenshots
* ✅ Share student results with permission
* ✅ Admit mistakes and failures
* ✅ Under-promise, over-deliver

**NEVER:**
* ❌ Fake screenshots
* ❌ Claim results you can't prove
* ❌ Use stock photos as "students"
* ❌ Exaggerate or lie about anything

**Trust is everything. One lie destroys it forever.**

---

### Rule 3: Students First, Revenue Second

**If you must choose:**
* Student success > short-term revenue
* Quality > quantity
* Completion rate > enrollment numbers
* Testimonials > cash

**Why:** Happy students = testimonials = referrals = long-term revenue

**One happy student who gets promoted = 5 referrals = Rp 7.5M**  
**One unhappy student = bad review = kills trust = -Rp 50M+ in lost sales**

---

### Rule 4: Stay in Your Lane

**Teach what you KNOW:**
* ✅ The Kamal Way framework (your IP)
* ✅ LLM fundamentals (you understand this)
* ✅ Prompt engineering (you've mastered this)
* ✅ Context management (you use this daily)
* ✅ Career strategy (you've lived this)

**Don't teach what you DON'T know:**
* ❌ How to build SaaS (not your expertise)
* ❌ Advanced ML/AI engineering (beyond scope)
* ❌ How to quit your job and travel (you're still working)
* ❌ High-ticket sales (different model)

**Stay authentic. Teach from experience, not theory.**

---

### Rule 5: Long-term Thinking

**This is a marathon, not a sprint.**

**Don't:**
* ❌ Burn out trying to hit $10K in Month 1
* ❌ Sacrifice PM job quality (you need credibility)
* ❌ Neglect family/health for business
* ❌ Scale before you're ready (quality drops)

**Do:**
* ✅ Build sustainable systems
* ✅ Rest when needed
* ✅ Celebrate small wins
* ✅ Remember: "L - Lifelong Project"

**You're building for 10,000 students over 3-5 years, not 1,000 in 6 months.**
