# Labeling Yourself — Magnifying the Problems

> [!tip] Compressed Idea
> - Problems magnify when you zoom in on them.
> - Your problems seem big when you zoom in on them. Your problems seem small when you zoom out from them.
> - You can solve most of your problems by zooming out and realizing how small it really is in the grand scheme of things.
