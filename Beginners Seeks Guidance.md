[Original date: 31 Jul, 2024 06:00]

> [!tip] Compressed Idea
> A guidance is necessary. Too much guidance is bad.

# Octa-blocks

> [!note] Topic
> - A guidance is necessary for a beginner
> - Too much guidance is bad for a beginner
> - An app that is too difficult to use doesn't mean it's good because customisability is not user friendly. You can literally choose any direction all by yourself and that's what make your life harder.

> [!fail] Problem
> - Many people who wants to start find it difficult to start because there are so many ways we can choose
> - Yet they still need guidance from seniors
> - But they don't neet too much guidance from too much seniors because it will distort they way of thinking

> [!success] Benefit
> - Knowing this, we can start early after we know a little trajectory to pursue
> - Knowing this, we can limit customisability from our service/product because it kills beginners' will too learn
