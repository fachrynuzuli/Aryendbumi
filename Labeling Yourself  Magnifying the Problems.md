- Labels form your identity
- all human understanding are metaphorical
- Attachment to these labels are what cause suffering (buddhism)

# Framework

- Accurate perception of the situation
- Accurate and advantageous shift in perspective (Zooming out)
- Repetition and practice for a lifetime
